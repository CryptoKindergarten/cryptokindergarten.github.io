So what is TLDR? TLDR is an acronym. An acronym is a word or phrase formed from the initial letters of a longer name or phrase, usually in a way that makes it easier to remember or refer to. Acronyms are often pronounced as a single word and are typically created by taking the first letter of each word in a multi-word name or phrase. TLDR stands for that stands for Too Long, Didn't Read.
